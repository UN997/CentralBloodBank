Privacy Policy



Central Blood Bank Privacy Policy


The Central Blood Bank is very much aware of how important it is to safeguard your privacy and insure the proper handling of your personal information. Almost all Central Blood Bank web pages can be accessed without giving us any information about yourself. Sometimes though we do require information to provide services that you request, and this privacy statement explains how data is collected and used in those situations. This privacy statement only applies to websites owned by the Central Blood Bank. It does not apply to other online sites, offline sites, sponsors, advertisers, products or services that may appear via links, articles, images and by other means on any Central Blood Bank website.


How We Collect Your Personal Information


We sometimes require your personal information so that we can identify you or contact you if necessary. Typically this is only required when you are using one of our online services like requesting for blood, registring as a donor, looking for nearby blood banks. When you register as a donor then we require your personal information only once when you are creating your account.


How We Use Your Personal Information


Your personal information is used in the following ways:

  • To identify you in our records.
  • To contact you if necessary.
  • To provide services to you that require your personal information.
  • To allow you access to otherwise restricted areas of a website.

We track and analyze your anonymous visit data so that we can research website usage trends to provide you with a better web surfing experience on our websites. None of your personal information is involved in this process.

The Central Blood Bank may need to disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in a good-faith effort to:
(a) adhere to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process;
(b) protect or defend the rights of Central Blood Bank property, or
(c) respond during circumstances that require immediate action to protect the personal safety of Central Blood Bank employees, donors, or members of the public in general.

The Central Blood Bank will never sell or otherwise distribute your information except as previously mentioned.


How We Control Your Personal Information


When you provide us with your personal information we will not share that information with parties outside of the Central Blood Bank without your permission other than when required to do so as outlined above. We will only use your personal information for the reasons mentioned above.


Registering an account at a Central Blood Bank website also allows you to specify your contact preferences so that we can make certain not to invade your privacy, but instead only provide you with the information you wish to receive and nothing more. Currently we have services in place which allow you to control your e-mail contact preferences via our website. I


How We Access Your Personal Information


The Central Blood Bank provides you with a variety of methods to ensure that your information is accurate and up to date.


If you register an online account using a Central Blood Bank website you are required to enter your personal information. During future visits and usage of that online account you will be given the opportunity to review and make changes to your information. During any donation process you will also be given the chance to review the information we have on record for you and update it where necessary. You may also contact us directly and request that any of your information be updated.
